About AMDM

The American Moeezland Diplomatic Mission (AMDM) serves as Moeezland’s official diplomatic presence inside the Kasimid Sultanate, located in Central New Jersey, United States. This mission acts as the central hub for Moeezland’s outreach to U.S.-based micronations, fostering communication, cooperation, and mutual understanding.

Purpose of AMDM

• Build and maintain diplomatic ties with U.S. micronations.
• Represent Moeezland in American regional affairs.
• Promote cooperation, cultural exchange, and peaceful relations.
• Serve as the contact point for American micronations.
• Support the Pak Commonwealth of Micronations (formally Alliance of Pakistani Micronations).

Location & Jurisdiction

AMDM operates from Moeezland’s Embassy located inside the Kasimid Sultanate in Central New Jersey. Its jurisdiction covers all diplomatic involvement within the United States micronational community.

Diplomatic Outreach

AMDM may establish formal contact for treaties, cooperative projects, cultural initiatives, and diplomatic correspondence. The mission ensures Moeezland’s interests are represented internationally while respecting regional protocols and maintaining peaceful relations.

The American Moeezland Diplomatic Mission (AMDM) serves as the official representation of Moeezland within the United States. It maintains interstate micronational relations, supports foreign policy operations, and upholds the diplomatic values of the Moeezland Government and the Pak Commonwealth of Micronations (formally Alliance of Pakistani Micronations).
